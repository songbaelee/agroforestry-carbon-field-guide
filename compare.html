Methodology v0.1

Organizations are compared primarily within the same functional role, using a common set of research dimensions that describe how they participate in the agroforestry carbon ecosystem. The dimensions are intended to support exploration and comparison, not to produce definitive rankings.

Initial values were drafted with the assistance of OpenAI ChatGPT (GPT-5.5) using publicly available information, the Field Guide taxonomy, and the research already captured in organization profiles. Each value is manually editable and expected to evolve as profiles are expanded, sources are reviewed, and better evidence is added.

Research status indicates confidence in the underlying profile: Complete means the organization has a sourced research profile, Expanding means the profile is being developed, and Preliminary means the comparison values are an early directional estimate.

Compare is generated from data/organizations.json. Comparable organizations are role-filtered; collaboration links are mapped separately in Ecosystem.
