Reading organization relationships from data/organizations.json.

Methodology note. Relationships are directional research judgments stored in data/organizations.json. Common collaborators identify organizations that appear together in projects, finance, MRV, certification, or delivery. Comparable peers identify organizations that are useful to evaluate side by side, usually within the same primary role.
Ecosystem is generated from data/organizations.json. The map is a living research layer, not a complete market census.
